Famous Astronauts

Meet the explorers who dared to journey beyond Earth and rewrite the story of space travel.

Neil Armstrong

First human to walk on the Moon during the Apollo 11 mission in 1969. His words, "That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind," became historic.

Kalpana Chawla

The first woman of Indian origin in space. She flew aboard the Space Shuttle Columbia in 1997 and again in 2003, where she tragically lost her life during re-entry.

Sunita Williams

A record-setting astronaut who has spent over 320 days in space. Known for her spacewalks and long-duration missions aboard the ISS.

Yuri Gagarin

The first human to journey into space aboard Vostok 1 in 1961. A Soviet hero, his successful orbit of Earth marked the dawn of human spaceflight.

Chris Hadfield

A Canadian astronaut known for his musical videos aboard the ISS and engaging space education. He became a cultural icon for bringing space closer to Earth.

Buzz Aldrin

Lunar module pilot on Apollo 11 and the second person to walk on the Moon. A vocal advocate for Mars exploration and continued space exploration.
